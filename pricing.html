FAQ

Questions answered.

Yes, absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any moment from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, and any unused credit is prorated toward your next billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current period.

Your data is always safe with us. If you downgrade to the Free plan and have more countdowns than the limit allows, your older items will be archived — not deleted. They'll be fully restored the moment you upgrade again. We never delete user data without explicit confirmation from you.

Premium and Master plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You'll experience the full plan with zero restrictions. At the end of the trial, you'll be prompted to add a payment method to continue — or you'll automatically drop back to the Free plan with no charge.

Master subscribers get a dedicated public page at /{your-username}_retrocountdowns/ — a personal hub where you can display your public countdowns, write a bio, add links, and curate a collection of live timers. It's your public identity on RetroCountdowns, styled with your custom theme and fonts.

Master plan users can choose from our curated library of 40+ premium typefaces — from elegant serifs to modern grotesks — to apply across their personal page, countdown cards, and embedded widgets. Your chosen font is saved to your theme so it travels consistently across every touchpoint of your RetroCountdowns presence.

On the Master plan you can save multiple named theme configurations — color palettes, font choices, layout preferences — and switch between them or restore a previous look at any time. This is ideal if you maintain multiple projects or brand identities, or if you simply love experimenting and want a reliable fallback.
